Questions, answered.

Do I need an existing audience?
No. Promote.dev gives you complete posts you can publish from a new or established account. Every source crossed the high-reach view threshold and passed the spoken-hook quality screen, but no tool can guarantee views or conversions.
What is included in each post?
Six designed 4:5 slides, a photographic cover where appropriate, a complete caption, niche-relevant hashtags, and a product call to action. Download the slides and caption together as a ready-to-publish ZIP. Pro can also turn the same post into a vertical Reel or publish it to Instagram.
Do I have to show my face or film anything?
Never. Slides are fully designed text-and-visual images. Reel export adds gentle movement and transitions without filming or editing; you choose the music when you upload it.
What makes a hook “proven”?
Promote.dev researches recent short-form videos and keeps only relevant English openings from videos with at least 250,000 views. Each opening must be confirmed near the start of the transcript and pass a separate quality and transferability screen.
Does it post to my accounts for me?
Yes. Connect Instagram once and Pro keeps a fresh daily post queued, shows you what is scheduled, and records whether publishing succeeded. You can still download and publish any post yourself.
How do Reel downloads work?
Pro turns a finished post into a silent 9:16 MP4 with slow movement and readable timing. Download it, upload it to Instagram, and choose a song from Instagram’s music library.
What do I need to connect Instagram?
A free Instagram Professional or Creator account — a one-tap switch in the Instagram app, and the standard requirement for any tool that posts for you. Link it once from your account page and each daily post goes up on its own.
Can I cancel?
Anytime, from your account page. No contracts, no minimums.
