Questions, answered.

Do I need followers for this to work?
No — that’s the point. Carousels and short-form get pushed by discovery algorithms, not your follower graph. Every hook in the feed came from a video that outperformed its account’s size by at least 5×.
What exactly do I get per carousel?
Five to six designed image slides (4:5), a written caption, hashtags, and a product call to action. Download everything as a post-ready ZIP. Pro can also turn the same slides into a vertical Reel or publish a fresh carousel to Instagram daily.
Do I have to show my face or film anything?
Never. Slides are fully designed text-and-visual images. Reel export adds gentle movement and transitions without filming or editing; you choose the music when you upload it.
Where do the hooks come from?
A miner scans short-form daily, keeps only statistical outliers (views ≥ 5× followers), and extracts the hook as a reusable template. Every hook links its source video and numbers — receipts included.
Does it post to my accounts for me?
Instagram auto-posting is in beta now. Connect your account once and Pro keeps a daily carousel queued, shows you what is scheduled, and records whether publishing succeeded. You can still download and post anything yourself.
How do Reel downloads work?
Pro turns a finished carousel into a silent 9:16 MP4 with slow movement and readable timing. Download it, upload it to Instagram, and choose a song from Instagram’s music library.
What do I need to connect Instagram?
A free Instagram Professional or Creator account — a one-tap switch in the Instagram app, and the standard requirement for any tool that posts for you. Link it once from your account page and every daily carousel goes up on its own.
Can I cancel?
Anytime, from your account page. No contracts, no minimums.
